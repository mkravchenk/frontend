The Infinity Stones

Name Color Powers and capabilities Image
Soul Orange Soul Gem gives the owner the power to alter and manipulate all souls. It also acts as a gateway to its own pocket dimension. When used in conjunction with the other gems, it gives the user complete control over all life in the universe.
Time Green Time Gem manipulates time, can amplify or reverse aging, and allows the user to see, travel into, and/or change the past or the future.
Space Blue the Space Gem was a purple Infinity Gem that increases the speed of its user and allows them to be teleported wherever they want.
Mind Yellow Mind Gem gives the owner psionic powers like telepathy and telekinesis or can augment the pre-existing mental powers of its user. When used in conjunction with the other gems, it can link its user with all other minds in the universe at once.
Reality Red The Reality Stone gives the user the ability to control dark-forces and at full power, manipulate reality. It can also revive the dead. It can even restore lost body parts of its hosts.
Power Purple As the Power Stone is one of the six Infinity Stones, it holds tremendous levels of great supernatural power that makes it the most powerful artifact of all creation. It's power could only be wielded by beings of immense power and can destroy planets.
